Viking Days 2015


Saturday and Sunday, August 22-23, 2015


FREE ADMISSION!


Join us for Viking Days! This family-friendly festival features delicious Nordic foods, lively entertainment, a Viking Encampment, and so much more!


For the second year in a row, we're kicking off Viking Days with the Run Like A Viking 5K!


Viking Days Festival


SCHEDULE:


Saturday, August 22


Run Like a Viking 5K: 8:00 a.m. start
Swedish Pancake Breakfast: 9:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m.
Festival: 10:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m.
Salmon BBQ: 4:30 p.m. – 7:00 p.m.


Sunday, August 23


Swedish Pancake Breakfast: 9:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m.
Festival: 10:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m.


Festival Highlights Include:


Swedish Pancake Breakfast both mornings
Alder-smoked salmon and live music at the Salmon BBQ on Saturday evening only
Goodies all day from the Scandinavian Food Hearths
Grilled sausages, craft beer, and aquavit in the Valhalla Beer Garden
Entertainment featuring Nordic music, craft demonstrations, vendors, and children’s activities
Larger-than-life Viking Encampment with weaving, weapon-forging, and war!


Run Like a Viking 5K


Dress in your Viking best for this race at nearby Golden Gardens Park. Is your pup fit to be a Viking hound? On-leash dogs are welcome to run --- and dress up --- too! Afterward, join us at the Museum for all your Viking Days favorites: Salmon BBQ, craft beer, Nordic music, crafts, and children's activities.


Gun Time for the race is Saturday, August 22 at 8:00 a.m.

The run will be chip timed and the field is limited to 500 participants. Packet Pick-Up is Friday, August 21, from 3:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m. at the Museum, and the morning of the race starting at 7:00 a.m. The course map is below.


Join us for Viking Days at the Nordic Heritage Museum following the race!


You can register as a group and kids 12 and under are invited to participate!


Got a group of friends you want to run with? A workout group that will challenge to succeed? Why not sign up as a group! We're also thrilled to welcome kids to the race at a special price. Kid-Vikings will receive a medal (not a t-shirt, sorry!) even if they don't make it to the end of the course. Kid costumes are highly encouraged.


T-Shirt, Goodie bag, and Inaugural Run Like a Viking 5K Finisher's Medal are included with regular registration!


Prizes will be awarded for best costume and best dog costume. Strollers and stroller-pushers are welcome to participate. Not so much a runner? You're more than welcome to walk the 5K! Register as if you were running, but take your time the day of the race.


Questions? Contact the Nordic Heritage Museum at (206) 789-5707
